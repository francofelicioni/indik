ABOUT US

Two friends - A life together - Mutual inspiration

Damian Armando Dreise , born in the city of Buenos Aires in the 80s. He began to take his first artistic steps as a child, standing out for his skill with the pencil. In his adolescence, he underwent a gender metamorphosis, a duality that would be reflected in his art in later years.

The need to externalise this internal change led him to take up the paintbrush for the first time, to express openly on canvas his experiences, existential doubts and most visceral feelings. Completely self-taught, he begins a process full of solitude, observation, learning and understanding, from which intense, intimidating and profound works emerge. This dark stage of self-knowledge came to an end with the appearance of light and colour in its maximum expression. His work includes oils, acrylics, watercolours and digital designs faithful to his essence and his very particular aesthetics. Today we present an open-minded tour through a selection of works that invite us to release preconceptions, to let ourselves be carried away by the irrationality of madness.
